# Salt & Sage — Design Brainstorm

## Approach A — Organic Modernism
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Organic Modernism / Editorial Naturalism
**Core Principles:**
- Earthy warmth grounded in natural tones (sage, salt-white, warm charcoal)
- Asymmetric editorial layouts with generous whitespace
- Typographic hierarchy using a high-contrast serif/sans pairing
- Subtle botanical motifs as decorative accents

**Color Philosophy:** Salt (off-white linen) and Sage (muted green) as the two anchors. Charcoal for body text. A warm gold accent for CTAs and highlights. Evokes trustworthiness, calm authority, and natural intelligence.

**Layout Paradigm:** Offset grid — headlines bleed left, content blocks stagger right, creating visual rhythm without symmetry. Sections separated by thin rules and generous vertical breathing room.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rule dividers with small botanical icon insets
- Large display numerals (01, 02, 03) as section markers
- Subtle grain/noise texture overlaid on section backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy:** Slow, deliberate reveals. Content fades and slides in from below as user scrolls. Hover states use gentle underline animations rather than color fills.

**Animation:** Staggered fade-up on scroll entry. Nav links have a slow underline draw animation. Section transitions use a subtle parallax offset.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif, bold italic for headlines)
- Body: DM Sans (clean, modern, readable)
- Accent: DM Sans Italic for pull-quotes
</text>

## Approach B — Refined Institutional
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Style meets Contemporary Consulting
**Core Principles:**
- Strict typographic grid with deliberate rule-breaking for emphasis
- Monochromatic base (near-black, off-white) with a single sage-green accent
- Data-forward layout language: numbers, labels, structured lists
- Restrained use of imagery; content is the visual

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#1a1a18) backgrounds for hero sections, off-white (#f5f2ec) for content areas. Sage green (#7a9e7e) as the sole accent. Creates a sense of institutional authority and intellectual rigor.

**Layout Paradigm:** Two-column editorial grid. Left column anchors labels/numbers; right column carries the content. Navigation is a fixed left sidebar on desktop.

**Signature Elements:**
- Oversized section labels in light-weight uppercase tracking
- Thin 1px sage-green vertical rules separating columns
- Numbered service cards in a strict grid

**Interaction Philosophy:** Precision over flair. Hover states reveal sage-green underlines. Scroll-triggered counters animate numbers. Minimal but intentional.

**Animation:** Clip-path reveals on section entry. Number counters animate on scroll. Subtle horizontal slide for card hovers.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (high-contrast serif for authority)
- Body: IBM Plex Sans (technical clarity)
- Labels: IBM Plex Mono (small caps, tracking)
</text>

## Approach C — Warm Editorial Luxury
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Warm Editorial Luxury / Contemporary Brand Identity
**Core Principles:**
- Rich cream and warm stone palette with deep forest green
- Asymmetric hero with large typographic lockup and offset imagery
- Generous section padding; content breathes
- Premium feel through restraint: few elements, each weighted

**Color Philosophy:** Cream (#f8f4ed) as the canvas. Deep forest green (#2d4a3e) for primary text and key sections. Warm sand (#c9b99a) as a mid-tone accent. This palette signals premium, trustworthy, and grounded — fitting for a high-end advisory firm.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed alternating sections — cream and forest green backgrounds swap to create rhythm. Hero uses a split layout: large headline left, abstract image right. Services use a horizontal scroll or staggered card grid.

**Signature Elements:**
- Italic serif headlines in cream on dark green backgrounds
- Thin gold/sand horizontal rules as section separators
- Subtle sage leaf SVG watermark in section backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy:** Luxurious pacing. Smooth scroll behavior. Cards lift with a soft shadow on hover. CTA buttons use a fill-sweep animation.

**Animation:** Fade-up with slight Y-offset on scroll. Hero headline has a character-by-character reveal. Section backgrounds use a slow color transition on entry.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Libre Baskerville (authoritative serif)
- Body: Nunito Sans (approachable, clean)
- Accent: Libre Baskerville Italic for quotes and emphasis
</text>

## Selected Approach: **C — Warm Editorial Luxury**

Chosen for its premium, trustworthy aesthetic that aligns with Salt & Sage's brand identity. The cream/forest-green palette directly references the brand name, while the editorial layout conveys authority without coldness.
