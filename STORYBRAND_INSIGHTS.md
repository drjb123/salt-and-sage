# StoryBrand Framework Insights for Salt & Sage Redesign

## Key Principles from Donald Miller & StoryBrand

### 1. **The Customer is the Hero, Not You**
- **Current Issue**: Salt & Sage positions itself as the expert/hero
- **Fix Needed**: Reframe messaging so the dental practice owner is the hero, and Salt & Sage is the guide/mentor
- **Example**: Instead of "We transform practices," say "Your practice can reach 10 cases/week—here's how"

### 2. **Clarity Over Cleverness**
- **Current Issue**: Some copy is sophisticated but potentially confusing for busy dentists
- **Fix Needed**: Use simpler, more direct language. Dentists want quick clarity, not flowery prose
- **Example**: "Reduce overhead from 70% to 49%" is clearer than "Unlock operational efficiency through systematic optimization"

### 3. **The 7 Key Website Sections (StoryBrand)**
1. **Hero Section** - Clear value proposition (what problem do you solve?)
2. **Problem** - Articulate the pain point explicitly
3. **Solution** - Show how you solve it
4. **Guide** - Position yourself as the mentor/guide (not the hero)
5. **Plan** - Clear steps to work with you
6. **Success** - Show what success looks like
7. **Call-to-Action** - Make it obvious what to do next

### 4. **The Sales Funnel Mindset**
- **Generate Leads**: Website should attract ideal customers (dental practices doing 3-8 cases/week)
- **Drive Conversions**: Clear CTAs at every stage (Schedule call, Download benchmark, etc.)
- **Boost Revenue**: Upsell training, ongoing coaching, etc.

### 5. **Common Website Mistakes to Avoid**
- ❌ Talking about yourself too much (Salt & Sage's current issue)
- ❌ Focusing on aesthetics over conversion (we're doing well here)
- ❌ Not being clear about what you offer (partially an issue)
- ❌ Making purchase/engagement complicated (need clearer CTAs)

## Specific Recommendations for Salt & Sage

### A. Reframe the Hero Section
**Current**: "We don't just advise—we implement. Salt & Sage connects organizations with comprehensive support..."

**Better**: "Your practice can produce 10+ cases per week with a smaller team. Here's how dentists like you are doing it."

### B. Add an Explicit "Problem" Section
Before jumping to services, explicitly state the pain:
- "You're working 5 days/week but still feel behind"
- "Your team is growing but efficiency isn't"
- "You're spending 70% of revenue on overhead"

### C. Reposition as the "Guide," Not the "Hero"
- Current: "We identify inefficiencies and implement systems"
- Better: "We guide you through a proven 5-step process to transform your practice. You're in control; we're the experienced mentor."

### D. Simplify CTAs
- Too many vague CTAs ("Discuss Training Options," "Schedule a Call")
- Better: Clear, specific CTAs at each stage:
  - Hero: "See Your Potential" (to calculator)
  - After Benchmarking: "Schedule a 20-min Discovery Call"
  - After Case Study: "Get Your Practice Audit" (free assessment)

### E. Add a "Before/After" Narrative
- **Before**: 3 cases/week, 10-person team, 75% overhead, working 5 days/week
- **After**: 10 cases/week, 6-person team, 49% overhead, working 3.5 days/week
- **The Gap**: This is the opportunity Salt & Sage fills

### F. Strengthen Social Proof
- Current: One testimonial (good) + case study (good)
- Better: Add 3-4 more short testimonials from different practice types
- Add: "Results from 47 dental practices over 5 years"

## Navigation & UX Fixes

### Issue: Users can't get back to home from secondary pages
**Fix**: 
- Logo on every page links to home
- Add "Back to Home" button in footer on secondary pages
- Add breadcrumb navigation: Home > Blog > Article

### Issue: Blog images are all the same
**Fix**: 
- Use 4 completely different images (one per article)
- Images should reflect the article topic (e.g., accountability = team meeting, data = dashboard, scaling = growth chart)

## Messaging Hierarchy for Salt & Sage

### Level 1: The Core Promise (Hero Section)
"Transform your dental practice: 3x production, smaller team, less overhead, more freedom."

### Level 2: The Problem (Why This Matters)
"Most dentists work harder each year but don't see proportional growth. Outdated systems and unclear accountability are costing you $300K+ annually."

### Level 3: The Solution (How We Help)
"Our proven 5-step process identifies inefficiencies, builds accountability systems, and trains your team—so you produce more with less."

### Level 4: The Guide (Why Us)
"We've worked with 47 dental practices. We know your challenges because we've solved them before."

### Level 5: The Plan (How to Start)
"1. Free practice audit (20 min call) → 2. Custom roadmap → 3. Implementation with ongoing support"

### Level 6: Success Stories (Proof)
"Dr. Chen went from 3 to 10 cases/week, reduced his team from 10 to 6, and now works 3.5 days/week."

### Level 7: The Ask (CTA)
"Schedule your free practice audit. See your specific opportunity."

## Conversion Optimization Tactics

1. **Reduce friction**: Make it easy to take next step (one-click to calendar, not a form)
2. **Specificity**: "Schedule a 20-minute discovery call" beats "Let's talk"
3. **Urgency**: "Limited spots available for Q2 engagements" (if true)
4. **Social proof**: Show results (# of practices, $ impact, testimonials)
5. **Scarcity**: "We take on 6 new practices per quarter"
6. **Free value**: Offer free audit, benchmark report, or template to lower barrier to entry
